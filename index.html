About CampusStay Hub

CampusStay Hub was developed to solve a real problem faced by Victoria University students: the lack of a centralised, reliable platform for finding affordable accommodation near campus.

Our platform brings together hotels, motels, and hostels within the Kampala areas surrounding Victoria University, giving students the ability to search, filter, and compare options instantly — with no page reloads.
